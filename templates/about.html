About Bird Classifier

In the last decade, Deep learning has emerged as the solution to the
problems that are easy for a human to understand but very difficult to put in a way so that computers can solve it. Image recognition is one example of such problems. Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) emerge as the best solution to image recognition problems. 

Results encouraged people to use CNN for audio classification. Because CNN operates on images rather than the audio, the first step is to transform audio data to image datasets. This is achieved by generating spectrograms from the audio dataset. Spectrograms of audio signals of different species shows different patterns and CNN can learn these patterns.

This model utilise the same technique. At first, audio signals are used to generate spectrograms and then a CNN model is trained on them.
